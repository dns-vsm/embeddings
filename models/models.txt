## Download pre-trained vectors
The pre-trained vectors for the DNS-VSM can be download from: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1NWGd-pfjZpsgASoZW7YUUiJFjWzEdozq/view?usp=sharing

Download and unzip the content to this folder.

The folder structure should looks like:

-> models
    -> ft
        -> 21epoc_minn11_maxn17
        -> 21epoc_minn11_maxn17.wv.syn0_ngrams.npy
